Hey, I'm Srikanth 👋

I'm a cloud engineer at AWS based in Austin, Texas. I've spent years building scalable systems and helping teams ship to production. When generative AI started changing how we build software, I wanted to learn it the way I learn everything — by building real things.

Over the years, I've helped organizations — from startups to enterprise — build cloud-native platforms that are resilient, efficient, and developer-friendly. Whether it's designing infrastructure-as-code pipelines, optimizing CI/CD workflows, scaling Kubernetes clusters, or integrating AI into real-world applications, I'm all about turning complex systems into reliable, automated solutions.

This blog is where I share what I build. Right now, I'm writing a hands-on series on building AI applications with AWS — each article is a standalone project you can clone, run, and learn from. No slides, no theory. Just real code solving real problems.

The series covers everything from your first Amazon Bedrock API call to production-grade RAG systems with governance controls. If you're a developer looking to get practical with generative AI on AWS, you're in the right place.

Let's build smarter, safer, and faster — together.
